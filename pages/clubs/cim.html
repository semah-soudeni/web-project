Our Mission

The Club INSAT Maintenance is the primary engineering unit responsible for the physical integrity of the campus. We execute critical repairs, architectural upgrades, and technical troubleshooting for all departments. Our crew specializes in rapid-response logistics and long-term infrastructure planning.
