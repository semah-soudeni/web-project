EMPOWERING STUDENT VOICES THROUGH JOURNALISM

By The Editorial Board

W

elcome to INSAT Press, the official journalistic body of our institute. For years, we have been the bridge between the administration, the clubs, and the student body, ensuring that every significant event is recorded, analyzed, and shared.

Our mission is to uphold the principles of truth, integrity, and objective reporting. Whether it's covering a major tech conference, interviewing a notable alumnus, or reviewing the latest campus play, our reporters are always on the scene.

“Journalism is what maintains democracy in our microcosm. We are the observers, the critics, and the storytellers.”

Join The Newsroom

We are always looking for passionate writers, meticulous editors, and keen photographers. No prior journalism experience is fully required, just a sharp eye and a dedication to the truth.

Our Departments

  • Editorial: Writing articles, opinion pieces, and interviews.
  • Photography: Capturing the visual essence of campus life.
  • Design: Formatting the magazine and digital content.
  • Social Media: Keeping the student body updated in real-time.
