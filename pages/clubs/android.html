Who We Are

Android Club INSAT is a student-powered tech hub at INSAT. We bridge the gap between classroom theory and real-world engineering across 6 major domains of computer science.

Whether you want to crack competitive programming contests, learn to secure systems, containerize apps with Docker, or train your first ML model — we've got you covered.
