About Our Club

Club Cine Radio INSAT is the ultimate destination for audiovisual enthusiasts. We are dedicated to the arts of broadcasting, podcasting, and filmmaking. Our goal is to amplify student voices, document campus life, and bring creative cinematic visions to reality.

What We Do

Radio INSAT

Tune in to our campus radio! We host talk shows, music hours, and interviews with fascinating people from our university.

🎬

Short Films

From script to screen. We produce short films, cover campus events, and explore the art of cinematography.

🎙️

Podcasts

Dive deep into tech trends, student life, and pop culture with our regular podcast episodes.
