We Serve. Empowering volunteers to serve their communities, meet humanitarian needs, encourage peace and promote international understanding.

About Our Club

Lions Club INSAT is a dedicated volunteer organization committed to making a tangible, positive difference in the lives of the less fortunate. As a proud chapter of Lions Clubs International, we channel the enthusiasm and skills of university students directly toward humanitarian service, environmental preservation, and community growth.

Our Focus Areas

👁️

Vision

We work to prevent blindness and improve eye health, honoring Helen Keller's call to become "knights of the blind."

🌍

Environment

Protecting and restoring our environment to improve the well-being of all communities.

🍎

Hunger

Fighting to ensure all members of our community have access to nutritious food.

❤️

Humanitarian

Responding to emergencies and providing ongoing support to the most vulnerable.
